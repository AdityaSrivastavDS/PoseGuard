About This Project

Driver Unwanted Pose Detection System is designed to monitor driver behaviour in real-time, detect distractions, and provide instant alerts to prevent accidents.

This system leverages computer vision and deep learning techniques to analyse live video feeds, classify driver activities, and detect unwanted poses indicative of distraction. When a driver is found engaging in unsafe behaviour, the system issues real-time warnings and transmits feedback to the transport facility owner or service provider for further action.

The project integrates various AI-powered modules, including image preprocessing, real-time video processing, distraction detection, alert generation, and remote reporting. By deploying this solution in cabs, transport services, and fleet management systems, organizations can enhance driver accountability, passenger safety, and operational efficiency.

This project aims to create a scalable, efficient, and cost-effective solution to minimize accidents, promote responsible driving, and contribute to safer roads.
